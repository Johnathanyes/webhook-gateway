## What is webhook-gateway?

webhook-gateway ingests inbound webhooks from providers (Stripe, GitHub, and
others), verifies their signatures, and reliably fans them out to your own
destinations with retries, dead-lettering, rate limiting, and replay.

## Core concepts

- **Sources** — inbound endpoints that receive and verify provider webhooks.
- **Destinations** — where verified events get delivered.
- **Routes** — rules that map sources to destinations.
- **Deliveries** — individual attempts, with retries and dead-lettering.
